History

As the product of two brothers’ shared ambition for boundless innovation, Potash Properties formed as a way to extend the principles of strategic design that have brought the team remarkable success in prior endeavors to the property industry.

Our mission

Potash Properties is concerned with more than just flipping multi-family homes, its focus is the refurbishment and revitalization of properties with potential and their communities. We aim to preserve the integrity of our assets while propelling them into the future through technology. Our approach is heavily influenced by the core principles of strategic design which transcend industry.

Team

With over 20 years of experience in contracting, augmented with successful ventures in many additional industries, our team is comprised of individuals who have demonstrated their ability to create value out of potential.

Marc Potash

Along with his leading role in Potash Properties, Marc Potash is the Founder and Managing Partner of Rolling Rock Ventures. He is also the Chairman and CEO of CERTIFY and was the Founder, Chairman & CEO of SecureNet.

SecureNet represents a classic “something from nothing” startup story that began in Marc's apartment back in 1997. In order to fund his tuition to Chiropractic school, Marc taught karate to children & adults. The studios ultimately grew to over 800 students and he needed a better way to bill his students. Thus, he set upon a course to build the world’s first web-based recurring billing application. SecureNet ultimately grew the recurring billing concept into an industry-leading payment platform. Tens of thousands of merchants have utilized the SecureNet platform, including Wells Fargo, GEICO, Ameriprise Financial and Schwan Foods.

Marc’s goal for Potash Properties has always been to extend the hands-on, innovative principles he has employed throughout his many roles to property ownership.
